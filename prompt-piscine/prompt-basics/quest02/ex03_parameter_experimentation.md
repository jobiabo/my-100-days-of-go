AI began as a bold dream in the 1950s: could machines think?

Early systems followed rigid rules.
Modern systems learn from data — especially through machine learning and deep learning.

But here's the twist:

 What AI Can Do Today

* Diagnose patterns in medical scans
* Recommend what you watch next

* Drive vehicles (partially or fully)
* Compose music
* Write essays
* Simulate conversation

 Why AI Feels Different Now

Three reasons:

1. **Scale of data**
2. **Computing power**
3. **Advanced neural networks**

 The Tension Around AI

AI is powerful — and power amplifies consequences.

It can:

* Reduce human workload
* Accelerate scientific discovery
* Personalize education

* Encode bias

* Disrupt job markets
* Raise deep ethical concerns

* What is intelligence?
