# Exercise 3: Multi-Step Question Answering
#### Objective: Break complex questions into smaller steps.

### Model Used:
  [OpenAi](https://www.chatgpt.com)

### Prompt:
- Restate the problem, Break it into smaller steps, Solve each step before giving the final answer
"What is 25% of the sum of 40 and 60?"

### Model Output with reasoning instruction:
  ### Step 1: Restate the Problem

We are asked to find **25% of the total when 40 and 60 are added together**.

---

###### Step 2: Break It Into Smaller Steps

1. **Find the sum of 40 and 60.**
2. **Find 25% of that sum.**

---

###### Step 3: Solve Each Step

**Step 3.1 — Add 40 and 60**

[
40 + 60 = 100
]

**Step 3.2 — Find 25% of 100**

25% means:

[
\frac{25}{100}
]

So:

[
\frac{25}{100} \times 100 = 25
]

---

 Final Answer

[
\boxed{25}
]

25% of the sum of 40 and 60 is **25**.
