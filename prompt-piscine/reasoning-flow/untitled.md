In all the exercise carried out, the basic steps of chain of thought start with clear understanding the question by identifying what is being asked and what information is provided. Next, break the problem into smaller, manageable parts that can be solved more easily. Then work through each part step by step, focusing on one small action at a time. As you move forward, ensure that each step logically connects to the previous one so your reasoning stays consistent and clear. Finally, after completing the reasoning process, the model state the final answer clearly and confidently.
